Snugged against the backdrop of western ghats, surrounded by greenary, rediscover what being one with nature is all about. At solitude, we focus on keeping you away from your routine life.

Our well-furnished cottage will provide you a wonderful stay while enjoying the beauty of nature around. (Maybe even rediscover yourself while you are at it ☺ )

Activities

We know some of you do not intent to stay inside the cottage the the whole day. Here are a few things you could do

Around the cottage

Have you seen how those spices like cardamom, pepper etc, are grown. Search no more, we have them right in our garden.

Waterfalls

Beautiful waterfalls to explore. Some, a short treck away and few a short drive away.

Anakulam

You may not be an elephant person, especially wild elephants. But once you see these magnificient ceratures, pause look around for humans and slowly advance to drink from this watering hole, you are not going to be able to wipe that smile off your face

Bridges

With numerous streams around, there are a number of old (well vegetated in some cases ☺ ) bridges around. Even a hanging bridge. Not for the faint hearted, but an epereince you will take back with you.
