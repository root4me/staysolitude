Snugged against the backdrop of western ghats, surrounded by greenery, rediscover what being one with nature is all about. In solitude, we focus on keeping you away from your routine life.

Our well-furnished contemporary cottage in the plantation will provide you a wonderful stay while enjoying the beauty of nature around. (Maybe even rediscover yourself while you are at it.). The elevated wooden cottage has a living room, a bedroom, dining and quite a big balcony on the top floor. Downstairs has an additional wood paneled bedroom. Top floor will offer you a view of the rainforests around and the bedroom down will keep you close to the nature with a view of the plantation.

Activities

We know some of you do not intent to stay inside the cottage the the whole day. Here are a few things you could do

Around the cottage

Have you seen, how those spices like cardamom, pepper etc, are grown. Search no more, we have them right in our garden. Enjoy the evening walk in the garden along the backyard pond and the natural streams running through the plantation.

Waterfalls

Beautiful waterfalls to explore. Viripara waterfalls nearby, offers a short trek. The majestic Kallar river which runs through the year would be a visual treat on your way to Solitude. You will get to see waterfalls right out your bedroom windows throughout the Monsoon.

Anakulam

You may not be an elephant person, especially wild elephants. But once you see these magnificent creatures, pause, look around for humans and slowly advance to drink from this watering hole, you are not going to be able to wipe that smile off your face.

Tea Estates

Tea gardens are synonyms to Munnar. Letchmi tea estate, near to Solitude is one of the best tea estates in Munnar. Enjoy the serene nature with a jeep ride or perhaps a walk along the garden in the fresh air
